Research Opportunities

Bat Ecology

Study the behavior, population dynamics, and ecological role of the fruit bat colony.

Ecosystem Services

Investigate the role of bats in ecosystem functioning and forest regeneration.

Habitat Management

Study habitat requirements and develop conservation strategies for urban wildlife.

Climate Change Impact

Investigate how climate change affects bat populations and their ecological functions.
