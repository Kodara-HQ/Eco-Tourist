Eidolon helvum - Straw-colored Fruit Bat

One of the largest fruit bats in Africa with wingspans up to 1 meter. These magnificent creatures are essential pollinators and seed dispersers.

Wingspan 75-100 cm
Weight 230-350 grams
Lifespan Up to 20 years
Color Straw to golden brown

Ecosystem Role

Fruit bats are often called "gardeners of the forest" for their crucial role in maintaining forest health through pollination and seed dispersal.
