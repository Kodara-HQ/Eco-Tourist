Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a guided tour?

You can book a guided tour by contacting us via phone, email, or WhatsApp. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance, especially for group visits.

What should I bring for my visit?

We recommend comfortable walking shoes, light clothing, a hat, sunscreen, and water. Cameras are welcome, but please be respectful of the wildlife.

Is the bat sanctuary safe to visit?

Yes, the bat sanctuary is completely safe. The fruit bats are harmless and do not pose any risk to visitors. Our guides will ensure your safety throughout the tour.

Can I conduct research at the sanctuary?

Yes, we welcome researchers. Please contact us with your research proposal and we'll help you obtain the necessary permits and access.

Are there educational programs for schools?

Yes, we offer special educational programs for schools and universities. Contact us to learn about our curriculum-aligned programs.

What is the best time to visit?

The best time to visit is during the dry season (November to March) when the bats are most active. Early morning and late afternoon are ideal for viewing.
