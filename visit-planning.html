Visiting Hours & Seasons

Opening Hours

What to Bring

Clothing

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Light, breathable clothing
  • Hat or cap for sun protection
  • Light jacket (for early morning/late afternoon)

Essentials

  • Water bottle
  • Sunscreen
  • Insect repellent
  • Small backpack

Photography

  • Camera (with extra batteries)
  • Binoculars (optional)
  • Memory cards
  • Tripod (for photography tours)

What Not to Bring

  • Loud music devices
  • Food (to avoid attracting wildlife)
  • Pets
  • Smoking materials

Eco-Guidelines

Keep Quiet

Maintain a quiet environment to avoid disturbing the bats and other wildlife. Speak in low voices and avoid sudden loud noises.

Stay on Paths

Follow designated trails and paths. Do not venture into restricted areas or disturb the natural habitat.

Respectful Photography

Take photos without using flash, which can disturb the bats. Keep a safe distance and follow guide instructions.

Leave No Trace

Carry out all your waste. Do not leave any litter behind. Help us maintain the pristine environment.

Follow Guide Instructions

Listen carefully to your guide's instructions for your safety and the protection of the wildlife.

Respect Wildlife

Do not attempt to touch or feed any animals. Observe from a distance and appreciate their natural behavior.

Getting Here

Location

Address: University of Energy and Natural Resources
Sunyani, Brong-Ahafo Region, Ghana

Coordinates: 7.3399° N, 2.3268° W

By Car

From Sunyani city center, take the road towards the university campus. The sanctuary is located within the main campus area.

Parking: Free parking available at the main campus entrance.

By Public Transport

Take a tro-tro (minibus) from Sunyani to UENR campus. The journey takes approximately 15-20 minutes.

Cost: Approximately ₵5-₵10

From Accra

Fly to Sunyani Airport, then take a taxi to UENR campus (approximately 30 minutes).

Taxi Cost: Approximately ₵50-₵80
